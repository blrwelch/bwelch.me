About me

Making complicated things make sense, from Fortune 500 retail to AI tools.

I'm a UI/UX designer with 17 years of experience. Most of what I do comes down to one thing: helping teams figure out what users actually need, then designing the product that gives it to them. That sounds simple, but it usually isn't. It's where I'm at my best.

I'm currently a Senior UI/UX Designer, where I'm collaborating with our team on an internal initiative to bring AI into how our teams work every day. In this new frontier, it is important to figure out how to make AI feel like a useful coworker instead of a (scary?) buzzword.

My research approach is built on collaboration. Projects are more successful when teammates feel like partners, not subjects. Pair that with a front-end background that keeps my designs grounded in what's actually buildable, and you get someone who can hold a real conversation with engineering, design with confidence, and ship work that lasts.
